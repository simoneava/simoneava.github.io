Thoughts and Prayers for Sale
Put your money where your mouth is.

My proposed internet intervention is a series of e-commerce listings for the ubiquitous "thoughts and prayers" that plaster the internet in the wake of mass tragedies. 

There has been much critique of politicians who serve up thoughts and prayers without taking any real action, and I imagine that the proceeds generated through any successful transactions could be donated to affected communities and organizations lobbying for change.

I imagine this taking place over ebay, craigslist, poshmark, FB marketplace, etc. I could also see it as a social media campaign that takes place on Twitter, like the concept of a "swear jar," there could be a call for politicians to donate everytime they use this phrase.
